## Lessons Learned

- **Numbers in Thai TTS must be written as words** — ElevenLabs v3 reads Arabic numerals unpredictably in Thai context. Always convert: 5:30→ตีห้าครึ่ง, 30→สามสิบ, etc.
- **Quiz question clarity requires both narration context AND choice distinctness** — a good narration that ends with "คุณรู้สึกอย่างไร?" fails if choices secretly test something specific (like business background). The question framing must match what the choices actually measure.
